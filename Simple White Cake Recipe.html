How to Bake a Cake

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 cups of sugar
  • 1/2 cup of butter
  • 3/4 cup of milk
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1/2 tsp salt

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Grease and flour a 9-inch round cake pan.
  3. In a large mixing bowl, cream together the butter and sugar until light and fluffy.
  4. Add the eggs one at a time, beating well after each addition.
  5. Mix in the vanilla extract.
  6. In another bowl, whisk together the flour, baking powder, and salt.
  7. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients, alternating with the milk, until everything is well combined.
  8. Pour the batter into the prepared cake pan.
  9. Bake for 30-35 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.
  10. Let the cake cool in the pan for 10 minutes, then remove from the pan and cool completely on a wire rack.
